## What this means

This is the first public ranking of the highest-Pc conjunction events in the Aerospace IVV verification dataset. The dataset was released in October 2025 as a diagnostic benchmark for SSA service providers — but until now, nobody had publicly extracted and named the highest-risk events.

Caveats (these are real):
- The TraCSS verification dataset is **explicitly diagnostic**, not operational (per User Guide §2). These Pc values came from the dataset's curated input ephemerides, not from real-time orbit determination of actual satellites today.
- High-Pc rows in the historical CDM and fictitious-edge-case ranges are by design — they were *engineered* to stress conjunction-assessment algorithms.
- Real Space-Track-catalog objects (Sat ID 5–62461) are flagged in the Classification column; their Pc reflects January 2025 orbital states.
